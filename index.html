I am a Mathematics graduate with a deep love for football and data. Here you will be able to find articles and analysis on football betting, unique (and sometimes quite silly) data visualisations, and my own propietary football statistics/metrics.

Football Facts and Stats That Interest Me

  • As of January 2024, James Milner had played against 2,277 players in the Premier League, equavilent to 48% of all PL players.
  • Across the top leagues, around 20-25% of all goals happen in the final 15 minutes.
  • Since 1998, over 60% of World Cup matches have ended with either a draw or a 1-goal margin.
  • Statistically speaking, teams do see a performance bump for around 3-5 games after hiring a new manager, including temporary appointments. However, performance usually reverts to the mean within 10 games.
